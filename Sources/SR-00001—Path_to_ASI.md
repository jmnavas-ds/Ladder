## Summary

The blog post proposes that ASI can be approached by emulating and scaling the human creative process: collecting ideas, combining them with randomness, testing against problems, and feeding results back into the top of the funnel. This is the same process that drove The Enlightenment and continues to drive innovation in places like San Francisco.

## Key Points

- AGI and ASI are on a spectrum of general cognitive ability
- The human process of progress involves 10 steps: experience, face challenges, solve, learn, talk, copy/modify/combine, sleep on it, new inputs, inspiration, repeat
- This iterative process can be orchestrated and scaled using current AI
- Testing is the hardest part — many solutions require real-world validation
- The process itself could serve as RL content for future AI training

## Connection to Problems

This source is the philosophical foundation for Ladder itself — the idea that systematic, looped ideation can drive progress toward superhuman problem-solving.

## Potential Ideas

- Automate each of the 10 steps as a pipeline stage
- Build an observatory to track which steps produce the best ideas
- Create scoring mechanisms for idea quality across iterations
